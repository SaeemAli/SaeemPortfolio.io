Services

As a data scientist, I can provide various services to make your life easier and give you insights into your data.

Data Visualisation

Creating graphs to help you visualise your data and make sense of it. Use them for reports or presentations to help easily understand large amounts of data.

Data Cleaning

Data can get messy and difficult to read and it can be tedious and time consuming to fix. I can quickly clean up data and make it much simpler to view. This will save you time and effort of manually fixing your data.

Data Analysis

I can use your data to find hidden insights which could be of great benefit to you. Correlations between seemingly unrelated topics, or the time of year that your profits are higher, I can help uncover them.

Machine Learning Models

Generate a machine learning model to make predcitions on your data. Can be used to classify data, predict the probability of an event occuring or predict numerical values.
